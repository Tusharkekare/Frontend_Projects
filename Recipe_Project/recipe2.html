  1. Spicy Yum-Yum Meat & Cabbage Wrap


    2. Ingredients
    1. s required Bacon, Onions & Cabbage, cooked together (leftovers welcome) as required Jalepeno Sausages, sliced and seared
    2. as required Flour Tortilla Wraps as required Garlic-Herb Roasted Diced Potatoes 1 cup Chicken broth or Bouillon as required Shredded Blended Cheese
    3. as required Yum-Yum Sauce as per need Optional: Garlic Powder, Chili Powder, Paprika (as desired) as required Optional: Crushed Red Peppers as per taste Optional Garnish: Olives and Cherry or Grape Tomatoes
    Cooking Instructions :
    1. After cooking Bacon, Cabbage and Onions together til desired doneness in the pan, add Sliced Jalepeno Sausage til Sausages are seared. May now optionally add Garlic Powder, Chili Powder and Paprika and Crushed Red Peppers.
    2. Add 1C of Chicken Bouillon to the meat and veggies in the pan and allow to simmer. Warm flour tortillas and line each plate with one tortilla wrap. Top with mixture from pan
    3. Sprinkle one side of loaded tortilla wrap with shredded cheese, the other side with roasted Potatoes.Fold wrap and flip over. Using a zig-zag motion, add the Yum-Yum Sauce and garnish with Herbs

  2. Welcome Cake


    3. Ingredients
    1. 2 eggs 2 cups flour 1 tesp baking soda 1 1/2 tesp baking powder 1/2 cup cocoa powder
    2. 1 cup castor sugar 1 cup brown sugar 1 tesp vanilla essence 1 cup Hot water 1/2 cup oil 200 ml Cream
    3. 250 grams Milk chocolate Sugar syrup 2 cup whipped cream Food colours as required Water
    Cooking Instructions :
    1. In a clean bowl add eggs, brown sugar, castor sugar, oil and vanilla essence and mix well.
    2. Now add sifted flour, baking soda,salt, baking powder and cocoa powder. Mix well.
    3. Add in greased required moulds and bake for about 25-30 minutes till done. Let it cool completely after baking and cut in layers.Soak cake layers with sugar syrup to moist. Mix melted chocolate with cream let it cool then beat it till fluffy and stiff. Now spread the chocolate cream in the layers of cake.
    4. Cover the cake with plain whipped cream. Place the cake in freezer for about 15 minutes. Then hold brushes make your own sceneries with food colours & water.Let's welcome new year with new spirit and new thoughts.

  3. Coconut Macaroons!


    4. Ingredients
    1. 4 egg whites 1/2 cup granulated sugar Pinch salt
    2. 3 drops Almond Extract (welcome to do without)
    3. 1 tsp vanilla extract/essence 4 cups dessicated Coconut, shredded
    Cooking Instructions :
    1. Line a tray with baking paper, preheat the oven on 150Â° In a bowl, add egg whites, sugar, salt, vanilla and almond if using.
    2. Beat the ingredients together until you get a frothy mixture. Should be about 2-3 minutes! Don't over mix. Add the Coconut to the egg white mix. Stir until combined!
    3. With a small ice cream Scoop or two tablespoons, Scoop the mixture into small balls. Place them on the tray a few cm apart Bake for 20-25 minutes, they should be golden brown. You may leave them plain or dip the bottom in chocolate. Both are delicious
